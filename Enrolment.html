Enrolment Process

Application for Admission: Required Documents

To apply for admission to Velabahleke High School, please ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Copy of the latest school report card e.g. Grade 6 report card.
  • Copy of the unbridged birth certificate.
  • Age- from 12 and 13 years old.
  • Contact details and ID copy of the parent.
  • Feeder school: Isikhwelo is considered first.

Application for Admission: Important Information

When applying for admission, please note the following:

  • Applications are open from 1st March to 30th September.
  • No late applications will be accepted.
  • All applications must be submitted in person at the school office.
  • Incomplete applications will not be processed.
