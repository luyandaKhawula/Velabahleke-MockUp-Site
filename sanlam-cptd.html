What is CPTD?

The Continuing Professional Teacher Development (CPTD) system is a professional development system for teachers in South Africa. It is designed to support teachers in their ongoing professional growth and development. The CPTD system is based on the principles of lifelong learning and aims to enhance the quality of teaching and learning in South African schools.

Sanlam is a registered service provider with the South African Council for Educators (SACE) and is able to provide CPTD points for teachers who complete their training. Educators are required to achieve tleast 150 points every 3 years by participating in various activities.

Sanlam offers a variety of activities through CPTD programmes that are designed to meet the needs of teachers at different stages of their careers. These programmes cover a range of topics, including classroom management, curriculum development, and assessment strategies. To find out more click here or scroll down.
