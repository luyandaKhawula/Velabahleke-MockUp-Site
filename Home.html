Principals' Welcome Message

Welcome to Velabahleke, where every child matters. We are about transforming lives. Velabahleke is also about addressing the imbalances of the past which were caused by the apartheid albatross.

The school offers a diverse curriculum and prides itself with the dedicated educators who do not only offer quality education to our learners, but also go beyond their call of duty with extra-curricular activities which seeks to instil leadership skills, values, and morals to our learners.
