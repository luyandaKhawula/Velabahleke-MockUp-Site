Woman's Month

This month of August we pause, to salute our victorious women. We remember the women of 1956, who marched against the injustice, discrimination and inequality that was imposed by the despotic government of the time. Today, we appreciate the sacrifices and selfless acts of kindness and love from women to fight against GBV and strive to empower each other in the fight against poverty and inequality. Imbokodo must rise victoriously.!!!
